Six of Crows by Leigh Bardugo

Plot Summary

Six of Crows is a fantasy novel set in the Grishaverse, a world created entirely by the author. After recieving an irrefusable offer to capture the famous scientist Bo-Yul Bayur in exchange for 30 million kruge, Kaz Brekker, leader of the infamous gang in Ketterdam known as the Dregs, recruits Jesper, Nina, Inej, Matthias, and Wylan to help him break into the Ice Court on a more than insane heist across Ravka; However, it proves to be much more difficult than he thought...
