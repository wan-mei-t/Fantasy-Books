These Violent Delights by Chloe Gong

Plot Summary

These Violent Delights is a Romeo and Juliette retelling set in 1920s Shanghai. Though it does keep some key aspects from the original Shakespeareane play, this book manages to perfectly mesh it into the political and cultural setting of Shanghai, which makes this story all the more interesting. It follows Juliette Cai and Roma Montagov, both part of rival gangs who fight for rule over Shanghai. But when murders start to spike on both sides of their rivalry, they'll have to put their feud aside to find out what is actually causing these deaths.
