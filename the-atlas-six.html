The Altas Six by Olivie Blake

Plot Summary

The Atlas Six is a story set in a more modern day. It follows 6 characters who get chosen to join The Alexadrian Society academy for magicians. However, there is a catch; after one year, only 5 of them will be able to continue their studies at the academy, while the other will be eliminated from the competition. So the real question is, how far are each of them willing to go to remain at the academy?
